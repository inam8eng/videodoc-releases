## What is VideoDoc?

VideoDoc turns a YouTube link or a local video into **one PDF your AI can actually see**: the full timestamped transcript plus the video's own key images, in order. Drop the PDF into ChatGPT or Claude and ask anything about a 3-hour video. Everything runs on your computer — nothing is uploaded.
